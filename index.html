About Me

Hi! My name is Andrew Babes. I am a first-generation Romanian College graduate with a degree in Computer Science From Depaul university in Chicago, Illinois.

I discovered my passion for engineering early on, which has allowed me to take full advantage of the opportunities presented to me and gain experience across a variety of environments, from small teams to larger organizations. I find a sense of balance and clarity in working with technology—designing efficient systems and exploring new tools brings a level of calm and focus that is difficult to put into words. For me, engineering is both a technical discipline and a creative outlet.

I am particularly interested in working with emerging technologies and data-driven systems. From building and testing AI models to collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data from multiple sources, I enjoy the entire process of turning raw information into meaningful insights. I value thoughtful system design, automation, and continuous improvement, and I enjoy solving complex problems that require both analytical thinking and creativity.

Beyond my professional interests, I am motivated by the idea of building things that make a positive impact. Whether through my work, side projects, or future business ventures, I strive to create solutions that contribute value and improve the world around me.

Outside of technology, I enjoy maintaining balance through activities away from the screen. I have a strong interest in cars—particularly German engineering—along with fitness, skiing, hiking, and traveling. Spending time outdoors and staying active is an important part of how I recharge and stay grounded.

I currently work at Eclipse Design Technologies as a Software Engineer, where I continue to develop my skills while building reliable, scalable systems.
